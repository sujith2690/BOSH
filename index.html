    Bosch Limited Annual report

    Bosch and its employees have shown immense strength in the recent tough situation. While Bosch India with its continuous actions and communication approach kept employees informed about the situation, the employees played their part by being patient and safe. This year’s Annual Report is special as the company was certified ‘Great Place to Work’ despite of such unprecedented situation. The business have shown immense talent and robustness to overcome hurdles. This has now been compounded by the dramatic worsening of the economic environment in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic. At the same time, we also demonstrate why people can trust our creative technologies. After all, trust is becoming a key quality factor in our “new normality.” People want to be assured t hat companies like Bosch are developing innovations for a more sustainable life.
